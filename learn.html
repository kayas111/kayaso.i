About Kayas
Kayas is a platform designed to connect or link various students to students or students to stationary businesses who have similar interests in a business aspect where potentials buyers of items are respectively connected to sellers of the items.
The platform serves to offer a service to those who have interest and intensions of acquiring flat screen televisions and woofers as well as those who intend to market off their televisions or woofers.

Other products and more so all products are offered through the platform which include;

  • Carpets.
  • Gas cylinders.
  • Wardopes.
  • Shoes.
  • Reading tables.
  • Television stands.
  • Racks, etc.
This is achieved through the connection of the two parties within the shortest time possible.
The platform is intended to serve students and for this reason,one needs to posses a valid University ID (Muk,Kyu or Mubs).

How it works
1.The party that intends to market off a television or woofer takes 1 to 3 clear photos of the item and the photos are sent for upload on the platform's display page.
NOTE:
The following details are required from the seller;
  • Name of the seller.
  • Student ID number.
  • Telephone contact.
  • Size of Tv screen and brand(optional).
  • Price of the item and
  • Location (Muk,Kyu or Mubs).

2. The potential buyer who is interested in an item clicks the contact button adjascent to the item.
NOTE:
The following details are required from the buyer;
  • Name of the buyer.
  • Student ID number.
  • Telephone contact and
  • Item ID.

NB:
Both parties are advised to verify each others details and therefore are advised to have their IDs with them during the transaction.
